About A-DAP-T

A-DAP-T is a pre-deployment AI-agent risk scanner
that scans uploaded projects or the public GitHub
repositories, identifies common agent deployment
risks, saves reports for logged-in users, and
provides a report-aware AI assistant to explain
findings and remediation steps.
